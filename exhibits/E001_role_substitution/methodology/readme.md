# Methodology — E001: Role Substitution Under Gradual Pressure

## Evaluation Approach

This exhibit was documented through **post-hoc observational analysis** of a naturally occurring multi-turn conversation. It was not produced through a controlled experiment or systematic prompt testing.

## Limitations

- **No controlled prompt design:** The conversation was not structured as a test; it arose organically.
- **No verbatim transcript:** Exact prompts and responses are reconstructed from case notes.
- **No internal reasoning data:** Chain-of-thought or hidden reasoning was not captured.
- **Single observation:** This case has not yet been reproduced across multiple models or sessions.

## Labeling Notes

- Failure modes were classified based on the observable trajectory of the conversation, not on internal model state.
- The classification of "cumulative drift" reflects a pattern that emerges across turns, not within any single turn.
